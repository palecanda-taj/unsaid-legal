Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

Unsaid is designed as a quiet reflection app to help users observe thoughts, emotions, and patterns. Your privacy matters to us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your email address, account details, saved reflections, responses, and app preferences.

Content you enter into Unsaid may be used to generate reflections, summaries, insights, and pattern observations within the app.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features, save your history, improve the experience, support authentication, maintain security, and improve app performance.

4. AI-Generated Content

Some features may use artificial intelligence to generate reflective responses. These responses are for personal awareness and informational purposes only. They are not medical, therapeutic, psychiatric, or professional advice.

5. Data Storage

Your information may be stored using trusted cloud service providers. We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Third-Party Services

Unsaid may use third-party services for authentication, analytics, payments, app performance, cloud storage, and AI features. These providers may process limited information necessary to support app functionality.

7. Children’s Privacy

Unsaid is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time. You may also request account deletion or data deletion by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
